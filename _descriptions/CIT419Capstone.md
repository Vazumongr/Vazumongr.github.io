projectName: Capstone
---

This project was done as the capstone for my Gaming and Simulation degree. It's top down, point and click movement shooter made in Unreal Engine 4 with C++. Randomized loot drops (models and stats). The only weapons are guns, point and click to shoot (essentially auto-attacks from LoL). Bullets hone in on the selected target. Can also build turrets using scrap (drops from enemies) which blast enemies with a laser beam. AI will chase the player if they get close enough, then attack once in range. If the player gets far enough away, they give up the chase. There is also a save system which will save the state of the world and can be reloaded later.
