projectName: ToonTanks
---

ToonTanks started out as a project as part of a UE4 C++ Udemy course. Top down, single player game where you drive a tank around destroying turrets. The course included tank movement, firing, and destroying of turrets and player. I added health drops from turrets, a scoring system, different turret types, and a remote leaderboard and account system. Leaderboard backend is handled using Amazon Web Services (API Gateway, Dynamo, and Lambda).
